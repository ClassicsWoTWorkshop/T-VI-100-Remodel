Thank you for downloading! To install, drag the two .wotmod files from the folder named with the vehicle option(s) of your choosing to <Your WoT Directory\mods\<Game Version>.

(Example: C:\Games\World_of_Tanks\mods\1.5.0.0)

Included are three versions of the remodel - one for the HT No. VI, one for the Tiger 131, and one for the Tiger I (where the remodelled 100mm replaces the long 88mm cannon).
